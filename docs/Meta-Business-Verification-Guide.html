  2. Go to business.facebook.com and log in with the account that owns the Jinyi Gold HK Business Portfolio.
  3. Click the “Make Updates” button in Meta’s rejection email, or navigate to: Settings → Business info → Business Verification.
  4. Before uploading, review the business name, address, and phone number fields in the form. Make sure they match the documents character for character:

  5. Upload the new document(s) for each rejected item.
